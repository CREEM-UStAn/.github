# Welcome to CREEM 👋

**Draft**

This site houses software and workshop development undertaken by members of CREEM, University of St Andrews

Welcome to the GitHub organisation of the **Centre for Research into Ecological and Environmental Modelling**, CREEM the University of St Andrews.

Our research focuses on statistical ecology. We develop methods, software, analysis workflows, and educational resources that support our research and enable reproducible science.

## What you will find here
- Research software and tools
- Data analysis pipelines
- Reproducible research workflows
- Teaching and training materials
- Open-source community projects

## Reproducible Research

Where possible, repositories associated with publications include:
- Source code
- Documentation
- Versioned releases
- Citation information
- Links to related publications
 
## Using and Citing Our Software

Please review the licence and citation instructions provided within individual repositories. Where available, use the repository's `CITATION.cff` file or linked publication when citing our work.

## Collaborating with Us

We welcome collaboration from researchers, students, industry partners, and the wider open-source community. Please open an issue or contact the repository maintainers if you would like to contribute.

## 🙋‍♀️ Contact
For further information about our research, please visit: [CREEM](https://www.creem.st-andrews.ac.uk/research/)
